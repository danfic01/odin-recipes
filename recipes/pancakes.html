Pancakes

Description

Here is the part of the page where I share a long, uninteresting story about my grandmother, neighbor or a celebrity who inspired me to make pancakes.

Keep scrolling, the recipe is way down below!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • baking powder
  • sugar
  • salt
  • milk
  • melted butter
  • egg

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the wet ingredients in a separate bowl, then add the dry ingredients to the wet ones and mix and stir until just combined (do not overmix). My secret: let the batter sit for a minute or two. It will allow the baking powder to activate and make your pancakes fluffy.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a skillet or griddle and once hot, drop two or three Tablespoons of the batter for each pancake, making sure the pancakes do not spread out. Cook for a minute, until fluffy. Flip, and cook on the other side for another minute.
