Fried Rice

Description

Here is the part of the page where I share a long, uninteresting story about my grandmother, neighbor or a celebrity who inspired me to make fried rice.

Keep scrolling, the recipe is way down below!

Ingredients

  • sesame oil
  • vegetable oil
  • cooked rice
  • soy sauce
  • frozen peas and diced carrots
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Cook vegetables. Grab a large skillet and pan fry the veggies in the oil until soft over high heat. Push the vegetable mixture to the side of the skillet.
  2. Scramble eggs. Add the eggs and scramble them in the pan.
  3. Stir in rice, soy sauce and other seasonings.
  4. Combine the rice and veggies together and cook until rice is heated through.
